Zomato Stock Price Forecasting

This project builds a forecasting pipeline to predict Zomato's short-term stock prices using both traditional ARIMA and Prophet time-series models. By comparing model performance, ARIMA emerged as the stronger predictor with an RMSE of 2.54 and MAPE of 2.53%, offering more reliable insights for investment decisions and risk management.
